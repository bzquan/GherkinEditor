Character Classes
\s
White space
\S
Not white space
\d
Digit
\D
Not digit
\w
Word
\W
Not word
\x
Hexadecimal digit
\O
Octal digit
\p
()*+,-.:;<=>?[]!"'#$%&{} (punctuation characters)
Modifiers
+
To match one or more times (eg \w+ or \d+)
*
To match zero or more times (eg \w* or \d*)
Groups and Ranges
.
Any character except new line (\n)
(a|b)
a or b
[abc]
Range (a or b or c)
[^abc]
Not (a or b or c)
[a-q]
Lower case letter from a to q
[A-Q]
Upper case letter from A to Q
[0-7]
Digit from 0 to 7
